Meet the learners!

  • Turtles:The Deep Diver

    They dive deep into anything they learn but sometimes they have struggle looking at the big picture during their deep dive.

  • Elephants:The Memory Maestro

    Excellent working memory they remember everything but can have difficulties applying the knowledge due to a lack of technique and over-dependency on memorization.

  • Owls:The Metacognitive Master

    They have the best knowledge of metacognition techniques but due to their vast knowledge of the techniques but they can't focus on which technique to apply at the required time.

  • Dolphins:The Social Scholar

    They are Playful, have social interaction, and adaptability, and can learn through engagement but most of the time they lack focus or depth in learning, rely on external stimuli for motivation, and have difficulty maintaining long-term habits.
